Privacy Policy

At CLEANZO, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting the information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your personal information when you use our website, mobile application, or services.

Information We Collect

CLEANZO may collect information including your name, phone number, email address, society name, address details, vehicle information, booking details, service preferences, and account-related information. This information is collected only for the purpose of providing vehicle cleaning and maintenance services, managing bookings, communicating with customers, and improving service quality.

How We Use Information

When you create an account, book a service, or contact our support team, the information you provide may be stored securely within our systems. We use this information to schedule services, verify bookings, provide customer support, process service requests, and send important service-related notifications.

Sharing and Disclosure

CLEANZO does not sell, trade, or rent customer information to third parties. Personal information may only be accessed by authorized CLEANZO personnel who require such information to perform operational duties, customer support activities, or service management functions.

Payments

Payment-related information submitted through the platform is used only for transaction verification and service confirmation. CLEANZO does not store UPI PINs, card numbers, banking passwords, or other sensitive financial credentials.

Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and operational security measures to protect user information against unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, or disclosure. However, no digital platform can guarantee absolute security, and users are encouraged to maintain the confidentiality of their account credentials.

Usage Data

The platform may collect limited usage information such as login activity, booking history, device information, browser details, and service interactions for security monitoring, troubleshooting, analytics, and service improvement purposes.

User Responsibilities

Users have the responsibility to ensure that all information provided remains accurate and updated. CLEANZO shall not be responsible for service disruptions caused by incorrect contact information, vehicle details, or address information provided by the customer.

Aggregated Data

CLEANZO may use aggregated and anonymized data for business analysis, operational planning, service optimization, and performance evaluation. Such information does not identify individual users.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect operational changes, legal requirements, or service improvements. Updated policies will become effective upon publication on the platform.

By registering an account, accessing the platform, or using CLEANZO services, you acknowledge and agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.
